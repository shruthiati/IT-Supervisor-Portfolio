Sample Helpdesk Ticket Response

Subject: Let’s Get Your Email Back on Track

Hi [User Name],

Thanks for getting in touch. I know how disruptive it can be when email stops working—especially when you're depending on it to keep things moving. Let’s get everything back up and running smoothly.

Here are a few quick checks to start with. It may take 5 minutes for any of these steps to work:

  • Internet Connection – Make sure you're connected and that everything's running smoothly on your end.
  • Login Credentials – Double-check your email address and password. If you're unsure, the “Forgot Password” link is a safe bet.
  • Email App Settings – If you're using an app (like Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.), confirm that the incoming and outgoing server settings match our email setup guide.
  • Webmail Test – Try logging in through [webmail link]. If it works there, the issue may be with your app or device.

If you’ve already gone through these steps or the problem persists, just let me know the following:

  • Any error messages you’re seeing
  • The device and email app you're using
  • When the issue began

With that info, I’ll be able to troubleshoot further and get things sorted out quickly.

Looking forward to resolving this for you!

Warm regards,
Shruthi Ati
